STEP 1 — OPEN CHROME EXTENSIONS PAGE
──────────────────────────────────────
  Open Google Chrome and go to:
      chrome://extensions

  (Copy and paste that into your address bar)


STEP 2 — TURN ON DEVELOPER MODE
─────────────────────────────────
  In the top-right corner of that page,
  flip the "Developer mode" toggle ON.


STEP 3 — LOAD THE EXTENSION
─────────────────────────────
  Click "Load unpacked"
  → Select THIS folder (the one containing this README)
  → Click Select Folder

  The FIRST-FIN icon will appear in your Chrome toolbar.
  (May be hidden — click the puzzle piece icon to pin it)


STEP 4 — LOG IN TO THE EXTENSION
──────────────────────────────────
  Click the FIRST-FIN icon in your toolbar.
  Log in with the same email + password as your platform.


STEP 5 — SCAN YOUR INVENTORY
──────────────────────────────
  1. Go to your dealership website's used inventory page
  2. Click the FIRST-FIN icon in your toolbar
  3. Click "Scan This Page"
  4. Choose ADD mode → click "Sync to FIRST-FIN"
  5. Open your platform → Inventory tab — your vehicles are there!

  Repeat whenever your inventory changes.
  Use REPLACE mode to do a full refresh.

NOTE: Do not click any .js files in this folder.
      The extension runs automatically from Chrome.
