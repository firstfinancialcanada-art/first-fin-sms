STEP 1 — LOG INTO YOUR PLATFORM
---------------------------------
  1. Open Google Chrome
  2. Go to:  https://app.firstfinancialcanada.com
  3. Log in with your email and password
     (provided to you by text message)


STEP 2 — COMPLETE THE SETUP WIZARD
------------------------------------
  The wizard opens automatically on your first login.

  Fill in each screen:
    - Your name and dealership name
    - Claim your dedicated SMS number
      (pick your area code > Search > pick a number > Confirm)
    - Set a permanent password

  If the wizard closes by accident:
    > Click Settings (gear icon, top right of platform)
    > Click "Re-run Setup Wizard" at the bottom


STEP 3 — INSTALL THE CHROME EXTENSION
----------------------------------------
  This extension does TWO things:
    1. Scans your website and pulls vehicles into FIRST-FIN
    2. Auto-fills Facebook Marketplace forms when you post

  3a. OPEN CHROME EXTENSIONS PAGE
      > In Chrome, type in the address bar:  chrome://extensions
      > Press Enter

  3b. TURN ON DEVELOPER MODE
      > Top-right corner of that page
      > Flip the "Developer mode" toggle to ON

  3c. LOAD THE EXTENSION
      > Click "Load unpacked"
      > Navigate to the "firstfin-extension" folder in this package
      > Click "Select Folder"
      > The FIRST-FIN icon appears in your Chrome toolbar
        (If you don't see it: click the puzzle piece icon
         in the toolbar > click the pin next to FIRST-FIN)

  3d. LOG INTO THE EXTENSION
      > Click the FIRST-FIN icon in your toolbar
      > Log in with the same email + password as your platform


STEP 4 — SCAN YOUR INVENTORY
------------------------------
  4a. Go to your dealership website's used inventory page
  4b. Click the FIRST-FIN toolbar icon
  4c. Click "Scan This Page"
  4d. Wait for the scan to finish (it crawls every page automatically)
  4e. Choose REPLACE mode
  4f. Click "Sync to FIRST-FIN"
  4g. Go back to your platform > click the Inventory tab
      > Your vehicles should all be there with photos

  TIP: Repeat whenever your inventory changes.
       Use REPLACE mode for a full refresh.


STEP 5 — POST TO FACEBOOK MARKETPLACE
---------------------------------------
  5a. In the platform, click the "FB Poster" tab
      > Your inventory loads automatically
  5b. Pick a vehicle from the queue on the left
  5c. Click "Auto-Fill Facebook"
      > A new Facebook tab opens with the form already filled in
  5d. Drag photos from the photo strip into the Facebook upload area
  5e. Click Publish on Facebook
  5f. Back in the platform, click "Mark Posted"
      > The next vehicle loads automatically

  TIP: Post up to 10 vehicles per day for best results.
       No extra software needed — everything runs in Chrome.
