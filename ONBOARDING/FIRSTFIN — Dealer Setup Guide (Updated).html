Getting Started
1
Log In to Your Platform
  • Open Google Chrome and go to app.firstfinancialcanada.com
  • Log in with the email and temporary password sent to your phone
Can't find the text? Call or text us: 587-306-6133
2
Complete Your Setup Wizard

Opens automatically on your first login — takes about 3 minutes.

  • Your Info — Dealership name, city, your name
  • Claim Your Phone Number — Enter your area code, search, pick a number, confirm. This is the number customers text to reach your AI assistant.
  • Set Your Password — Change from the temporary one to something permanent
Wizard closed accidentally? Go to Settings (gear icon, top right) and click Re-run Setup Wizard at the bottom.
Inventory + Facebook Posting
3
Install the Chrome Extension

This extension scans your dealership website and pulls all vehicles into FIRST-FIN with one click. It also powers the Facebook auto-fill feature.

  • Find the firstfin-extension folder in the files we sent you
  • Open Chrome and go to chrome://extensions
  • Turn on Developer Mode (toggle in the top-right corner)
  • Click Load unpacked and select the firstfin-extension folder, then click Select Folder
  • The FIRST-FIN icon appears in your toolbar — click the puzzle icon to pin it if needed
  • Click the FIRST-FIN icon and log in with your platform credentials
4
Scan Your Inventory
  • Go to your dealership website's used inventory page
  • Click the FIRST-FIN toolbar icon
  • Click Scan This Page — the extension finds all vehicles and scrapes photos, prices, mileage
  • When done, choose Replace mode and click Sync to FIRST-FIN
  • Go back to your platform and click the Inventory tab — your vehicles should all be there
Repeat whenever your inventory changes. Use Replace mode for a full refresh. The extension crawls every page automatically.
5
Post to Facebook Marketplace

No extra software needed — everything runs in Chrome.

  • In the platform, click the FB Poster tab
  • Your inventory loads automatically — pick a vehicle from the list
  • Click Auto-Fill Facebook — a new Facebook Marketplace tab opens with the form already filled
  • Drag photos from the photo strip in the poster to the Facebook upload area
  • Click Publish on Facebook
  • Back in the platform, click Mark Posted — the next vehicle loads automatically
Post up to 10 vehicles per day for best results. Use the Queue view for a daily batch, or switch to All Vehicles to pick any car.
