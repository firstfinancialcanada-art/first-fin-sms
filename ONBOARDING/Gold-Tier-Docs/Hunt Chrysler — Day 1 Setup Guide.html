Your Sarah Number — How It Works

Sarah is your AI receptionist. The number you pick in Step 5 is what your customers will see on their caller ID and what they'll text. Here's the model we recommend starting with:

One number for the whole team (recommended)

You, Robbie, Wes, and your 7 reps all share one Sarah number. Inbound texts and calls land in your shared CRM. Sarah handles the conversation, qualifies the lead, and routes to whichever rep is assigned (per your routing rules). Replies go out from the SAME shared number — your customer experiences it as a single seamless line, not 10 different reps texting them from 10 different numbers.

This is how 95% of dealerships start. It's simpler, cleaner, and customers prefer it.

Notifications go to YOUR personal cell

When Sarah books an appointment, gets a callback request, or escalates a serious buyer — she texts you on your personal cell (whatever number you set as "Notify Phone" in Settings). That way you see what's happening in real time without having to log in. Reps can configure their own notify phones too.

Want per-rep numbers later?

Optional add-on at ~$15/mo per rep. Each rep gets a dedicated Sarah number for their assigned leads. Useful if your team prefers to feel "ownership" of their leads from first contact through close. Don't worry about this on day 1 — start with one shared number, add per-rep numbers later if you actually want them. Most dealerships never bother.

Your Inventory — Already Loaded

Editing or deleting individual vehicles

Inventory tab → click the trash icon next to any vehicle to remove it. Click Use in Deal to pull it into the Deal Desk for a customer. Bulk-select with the checkboxes on the left → Delete Selected to clear groups at once.

Need a full inventory overhaul?

Adding new inventory page (e.g., Hunt's NEW vehicles)? Switching to a different lot? Importing 100+ more vehicles? Just call or email us — our development team can do a full overhaul remotely. No need to fight with the inventory controls yourself if it's a big job.

For day-to-day adds, use the Import CSV / Excel button at the top of the Inventory tab — drop in a spreadsheet and your vehicles populate. Click CSV Template next to it to grab the format if you need it.

Day 1 Quick Wins

Recommended order of operations once you're inside the platform:

Tip 1 · Get to know Sarah first

Send 2-3 single SMS messages from your CRM to a friend's phone. Watch how Sarah handles inbound replies. Get a feel for her tone. Before you turn her loose on real leads, you want to know exactly what she'll say.

Tip 2 · Set your GST / HST rate

Settings → Default GST Rate → change to 13 for Ontario HST. This auto-applies to every Deal Desk calculation.

Tip 3 · Try the FB Poster on 1 vehicle

Inventory → pick a vehicle → click FB Poster in the header → Auto-Fill Facebook. Watch all 14 fields populate from your vehicle data. Click Open All Photos, drag them into the FB Marketplace upload area. ~30 seconds per listing once you get the hang of it.

Tip 4 · Build a fake deal in the Deal Desk

Deal Desk → pick a vehicle → enter a fake customer credit profile → click Compare All to see every lender side-by-side with real-time rates.
