Quick Start Guide
Your platform is almost ready. Here's what to do next to get fully set up:
🚗
Upload Your Inventory
Go to Inventory tab → Import CSV or add vehicles manually. Sarah uses your live inventory to answer customer questions.
🏦
Upload Lender Rate Sheets
Go to Lenders tab → Upload PDF rate sheets for each lender. This powers the Compare All engine with your actual rates.
👥
Import Your CRM Contacts
Go to Analytics → CRM tab → Import CSV with your existing customer list. Use Bulk SMS to re-engage them with Sarah.
🖼
Upload Your Logo
Go to Settings → upload your dealership logo. It appears on the customer-facing Deal Presentation.
📱
Launch Sarah — Send Your First Outreach
Go to Sarah AI → Launch SMS. Enter a customer's number and Sarah will start the conversation automatically.
✅
You're All Set!
